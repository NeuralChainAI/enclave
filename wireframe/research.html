What's the median indemnity cap across our SaaS MSAs signed in the last 24 months?
Asked 14s ago · 47 sources retrieved · 6 cited in answer

Across 142 SaaS MSAs executed by Acme Legal between June 2024 and May 2026, the median indemnity cap is 12 months of fees paid 12, with the interquartile range spanning 6–18 months.

The distribution shifted meaningfully in Q3 2025: prior to that quarter, the median cap was 6 months 3; afterward it stabilized at 12 months. This corresponds to your firm's policy update circulated September 2025 4 raising the preferred floor.

Outliers worth noting:

· 8 MSAs carry uncapped indemnity for IP infringement claims 5, all with strategic vendors.
· 3 MSAs cap at "fees paid in prior 3 months", well below median 6 — flagged for review.

Comparison to market: external benchmarks place the SaaS-vendor-side median at 12 months as well, putting Acme's portfolio in line with industry norm.
