When this clause appears
Triggers on limitation of liability / aggregate liability provisions. Flags when the clause is missing, uncapped, or caps liability above 12 months' fees.

"…each party's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim."

Cap no greater than 24 months' fees. Anything uncapped, or above 24 months, must escalate.

Negotiation guidance & common objections
Counterparties often argue an uncapped carve-out for data breach. Acceptable to concede a super-cap (not unlimited) tied to cyber-insurance limits. If vendor refuses any cap, this is a deal-blocker — route to GC. Used as the basis for redlines on the live Apollo Industries MSA in Review.
